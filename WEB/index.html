About the Event

Join us for a vibrant Nigerian Cuisine Cookout, a celebration of rich flavors, culture, and community! This event brings together food lovers to experience authentic Nigerian dishes, from jollof rice to suya, all freshly prepared by talented cooks. Whether you're Nigerian, a fan of African cuisine, or just curious to try something new, this cookout is for you. Come hungry, connect with great people, and enjoy an unforgettable taste of Nigeria!
